Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 30, 2025

1. Information We Collect

Saylani Entry Test Practice does not require user registration or collect personal information. We operate on a completely anonymous basis.

2. How We Use Information

Since we don't collect personal information, we don't use, share, or sell any user data. All test results and progress are stored locally in your browser.

3. Cookies

We may use browser storage (localStorage) to save your test progress and preferences on your device. This data never leaves your device.

4. Third-Party Services

We do not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services.

5. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over it. Clearing your browser data will remove all stored information.

6. Children's Privacy

Our service is available to users of all ages. Since we don't collect any personal information, there are no special provisions for children's data.

7. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please use the contact information provided on our website.
