Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 2025

1. Introduction

Saylani Mass IT Training ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use the Saylani Entry Test Practice platform.

2. Information We Collect

Our platform is designed to require minimal personal information:

  • Usage Data: We may collect information about how you interact with the platform, including test scores and subject preferences
  • Technical Data: Browser type, device information, IP address, and access times
  • No Account Required: We do not require you to create an account or provide personal identification

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the platform
  • Improve user experience
  • Analyze platform usage and performance
  • Detect and prevent technical issues
  • Enhance our educational content

4. Data Storage

Your test results and progress may be stored locally in your browser using local storage or cookies. This data remains on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

5. Cookies

We may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

6. Third-Party Services

Our platform may use third-party services for:

  • Content delivery (images, fonts)
  • Analytics and performance monitoring

These services may have their own privacy policies.

7. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

8. Children's Privacy

Our platform is designed for educational purposes and may be used by students of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children without parental consent.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your locally stored data
  • Clear your browser data at any time
  • Opt-out of cookies through browser settings
  • Request information about our data practices

10. Data Retention

Since most data is stored locally on your device, you control how long it is retained. You can clear this data through your browser settings at any time.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any significant changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

12. International Users

If you are accessing our platform from outside Pakistan, please be aware that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in Pakistan.
