Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions

Is this practice test free?

Yes, all practice tests on our platform are completely free. We believe in making quality education accessible to everyone.

How many questions are in each test?

The number of questions varies by subject, but each test contains comprehensive MCQs to thoroughly assess your knowledge in that area.

Can I retake the tests?

Absolutely! You can retake any test as many times as you want. We encourage repeated practice to improve your performance.

Is there a time limit for the tests?

No, there is no time limit. Take your time to carefully read and answer each question at your own pace.

Will I see explanations for the answers?

Yes! After completing each test, you'll receive detailed explanations for every question, helping you understand the correct answers and learn from any mistakes.

Can I use this on my mobile phone?

Yes, our platform is fully responsive and works perfectly on all devices including smartphones, tablets, and desktop computers.

Do I need to create an account?

No account is required. Simply select a subject and start practicing immediately.

Are the questions updated regularly?

Yes, we regularly update our question bank to ensure you have access to the most relevant and up-to-date content.

How can I track my progress?

After each test, you'll receive instant results showing your score, correct answers, incorrect answers, and detailed explanations.

Who can use this platform?

This platform is designed for all students preparing for entry tests, particularly those applying to Saylani Mass IT Training programs.
