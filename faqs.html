Frequently Asked Questions

Is this practice test free?

Yes! All our practice tests are completely free to use. No hidden charges or subscriptions required.

How many questions are there in each subject?

The number of questions varies by subject. You'll see the total count when you start a test.

Is there a time limit for the tests?

No, there is no time limit. You can take as much time as you need to answer each question.

Can I review my answers?

Yes! After completing the test, you'll see detailed results including correct answers and explanations for each question.

Can I retake a test?

Absolutely! You can retake any test as many times as you want to improve your scores.

Do I need to create an account?

No account is needed. Simply visit the website and start practicing immediately.

Is the website mobile-friendly?

Yes! Our website is fully responsive and works perfectly on smartphones, tablets, and desktop computers.

Where do the questions come from?

Our questions are carefully curated to match the pattern and difficulty level of actual entry tests.
